Dysfunctional Entertainment

Dysfunctional Entertainment was founded in 2022 from the remnants of now defunct group ONE0 Media. Today we're a group of artists and friends doing things we enjoy and use the Dysfunctional name to publish our projects under a unified banner. Our point of pride is transparency whether it be our use of free and open licenses on our projects or willingness to upfront and honest about internal issues or problems with projects.
